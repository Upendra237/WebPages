Meditation Practices

🧘

Mindfulness Meditation

The foundation of all meditation. Simply observe your breath without trying to change it. Notice thoughts arising like clouds — acknowledge them, then let them pass. Return to the breath. Every return is a rep of mental fitness.

Best for: Beginners, daily practice, stress relief

💜

Loving-Kindness (Metta)

Direct warmth and compassion first toward yourself, then expanding outward: to a loved one, a neutral person, a difficult person, and finally all beings. Repeat silently: "May you be happy. May you be safe. May you be healthy. May you live with ease."

Best for: Emotional healing, compassion, relationship pain

👁️

Body Scan

Slowly move attention through every part of your body — from the crown of your head to the tips of your toes. Notice sensations without judgment: warmth, tension, tingling, numbness. Where you place awareness, healing follows.

Best for: Tension release, sleep, chronic pain, body awareness

🕯️

Open Awareness (Zazen)

Rather than focusing on one object, rest in spacious awareness itself. Let sounds, thoughts, sensations arise and dissolve without following any of them. You are the sky — everything else is weather. This is the practice of simply being.

Best for: Advanced practitioners, existential clarity, deep peace

🌊

Visualization

Create a vivid mental sanctuary — a place of complete safety and beauty. It might be a forest clearing, a beach at dawn, or a floating cloud. Engage all senses: feel the warmth, hear the sounds, smell the air. Your nervous system cannot distinguish vivid imagination from reality.

Best for: Anxiety, performance, creativity, emotional regulation

Community Building Bhaktapur, Nepal

Nawadurga Devghad Bhaban

A community hall project for the Nawadurga Devghad area in Bhaktapur a structure that serves as a gathering point for local community events, cultural programs, and public functions. Mistri Vai Engineering Club contributed AutoCAD documentation, structural consultation, and site-level expertise.

This project positioned the club as a trusted technical resource in the local community, demonstrating capability beyond classroom theory.

Kera Ghari Residential Building

Signed on 2082 Kartik 14, the Kera Ghari Residential Building is Mistri Vai Engineering Club's first formally contracted residential project a landmark milestone marking the transition from workshop-learning to full professional practice.

This multi-storey residential structure is being designed with structural analysis, detailed AutoCAD drawings, 3D SketchUp modelling, and construction documentation managed in-house by club members.

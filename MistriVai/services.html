Our Services

What We Offer

01

Civil Construction

End-to-end construction management from foundation to finish ensuring structural integrity, code compliance, and quality delivery on every project.

  • Foundation design & analysis
  • RCC structural design
  • Site supervision & quality control
  • Bill of Quantities (BOQ)
02

Architectural Design

Thoughtful spatial design that blends modern aesthetics with functional living from concept sketches and AutoCAD drawings to construction documentation.

  • Floor plan & elevation drawings
  • 3D SketchUp modelling
  • Interior layout planning
  • Building permit drawings
03

Structural Engineering

Rigorous structural analysis and design ensuring earthquake-resilient buildings critical for Nepal's seismic zone, following NBC and IS code standards.

  • Seismic analysis & design
  • Load calculations
  • Beam, column & slab design
  • Retrofit & strengthening
04

Interior Design

Creating beautiful, functional interiors that reflect personality and purpose from residential rooms to commercial spaces with thoughtful material selection.

  • Space planning & layout
  • Material & finish selection
  • Furniture arrangement
  • Lighting design
05

Landscape & Garden Design

Bringing the beauty of nature to your property curated garden designs, water features, outdoor pathways, and green environments tailored to Nepal's climate.

  • Garden layout & planting plans
  • Hardscape & pathway design
  • Water feature integration
  • Outdoor lighting
06

Construction Consulting

Expert advisory at every stage of your project from initial feasibility through to handover, ensuring compliance, cost efficiency, and quality outcomes.

  • Feasibility studies
  • Cost estimation & BOQ
  • Contractor evaluation
  • Progress reporting
07

Surveying & Site Analysis

Precision surveying using Auto Level and modern instruments topographic surveys, land measurement, and site analysis before construction begins.

  • Auto Level surveying
  • Topographic mapping
  • Boundary demarcation
  • Contour mapping
08

Water Supply & Sanitation

Design and consulting for water supply networks, drainage systems, and sanitation infrastructure in partnership with Pani Tech solutions.

  • Water supply design
  • Drainage & sewage systems
  • Rainwater harvesting
  • Sanitation facility planning

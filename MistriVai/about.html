Mistri Vai Engineering Club is a student-led civil engineering community founded in Nepal learning together, building together, and growing together.

Who We Are

A club born from passion for engineering.

Mistri Vai Engineering Club (मसतर भई इनजनयरङ कलब) is a registered engineering club based in Dhulikhel, Kavrepalanchok, Nepal. We are a group of young civil engineers, architects, and designers working together to learn, grow, and deliver quality work.

We run workshops, host expert sessions, collaborate on real projects, and build a community where engineering students and professionals thrive. Since our foundation in 2081 BS, we have grown from a shared idea into a registered organisation with a growing portfolio.

Our Journey

From idea to organisation.

2081 Fagun

Foundation & Instagram Launch

Mistri Vai Engineering Club takes its first steps the official Instagram page is created, marking the beginning of a community built on civil engineering passion.

2082 Baishak 28

Official Club Launch 12 Founding Members

The club holds its first official meeting with 12 founding members, establishing the vision, structure, and goals of Mistri Vai Engineering Club.

2082 Jestha 14

Second Meeting Growth to 15 Members

Membership grows to 15 active members plus 4 audience participants. The club establishes a monthly fund of Rs. 100/member to support future activities.

2082 Ashar 17

First AutoCAD Training MS Teams

The club's first formal training session: a 7-day AutoCAD class hosted online via Microsoft Teams by Er. Nishan Makaju, with 16 participants from across the club.

2082 Shrawan 13

Auto Level Demo Nawadurga Devbhadra Bhavan

A live site demonstration of Auto Level surveying equipment conducted at Nawadurga Devbhadra Bhavan by Er. Sharika Khulimuli blending theory with site practice.

2082 Ashoj 11

MOU with Narganga Engineering Solution Pvt. Ltd.

Mistri Vai signs a Memorandum of Understanding with Narganga Engineering Solution Pvt. Ltd., enabling the club to take on professional engineering work.

DashainTihar Interval

SketchUp Workshop by Ayush Singh

Chief Architect Ayush Singh leads a SketchUp 3D modelling workshop for club members during the festival interval expanding the team's design toolset.

2082 Kartik 14

First Residential Project Signed Kera Ghari

The club signs its first residential building contract: the Kera Ghari Residential Building project a milestone marking Mistri Vai's transition into professional practice.

2082 Mangsir 8

Welcome & Experience Sharing Batch 82

A welcome and experience-sharing programme for Batch 82 students, introducing new members to the club's culture, projects, and professional development opportunities.

2082 Poush 16

MOU with Pani Tech

A second MOU is signed this time with Pani Tech further expanding Mistri Vai's professional network and the range of projects available to members.

2082 Fagun 1

Acoustic Night Insome Recordz, Mama Gang & Friends

Mistri Vai hosts an Acoustic Night featuring Insome Recordz, Mama Gang, _The Archi_Kid, and Gen_Archiez celebrating community, creativity, and the arts.

2082 Fagun 8

Official Registration Regd. No. 15648/082/083

Mistri Vai Engineering Club receives official government registration (Regd. No. 15648/082/083, PAN 133885297) a landmark achievement cementing the club as a recognised organisation.

About Us

Mistri Vai Engineering Club is a Nepal-based civil engineering firm headquartered in Liwali, Bhaktapur with a branch in Dhulikhel-6, Kavre. We combine technical rigor with creative design to deliver spaces that inspire.

Founded by engineers and architects who believe every structure tells a story, we are committed to quality, transparency, and innovation on every project we undertake.

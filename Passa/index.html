Practice

Functional Minimalism
Layouts built around the natural movement of people — eliminating friction, honouring light.
Vernacular Sensitivity
Materials drawn from Nepali craft — mud plaster, slate, timber, tiki jhya lattice.
Interior–Exterior Dialogue
Courtyards and voids that dissolve the boundary between built form and open sky.
Sustainable Intent

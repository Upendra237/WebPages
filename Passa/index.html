About

Pasang Tamang

"Architecture should be a manifestation of a person's world view — the way in which it makes sense of the space of this world."

Simon Unwin — a guiding principle

I am an architectural student at Himalaya College of Engineering, driven by a passion for design innovation and sustainability. My approach combines creativity with a deep understanding of functional needs — aiming to create spaces that resonate with both present and future demands.

My practicum at Sarosh Pradhan & Associates was one of the most formative experiences of my life — not just as a designer, but as a person. Working across hospitality, residential, commercial and office typologies gave me fluency in the full arc from concept brainstorming to site supervision and bill of quantities.

Education B.Arch — Himalaya College of Engineering, Chyasal, Lalitpur
Internship Sarosh Pradhan & Associates (SPA), Jyatha Marg, Kathmandu

Our Story

Designed for companies
built for people.

Mistri Vai Engineering Club was founded with a singular vision: to elevate the standard of civil construction and architectural design in Nepal. Headquartered in Liwali, Bhaktapur, with a branch in Dhulikhel-6, Kavre, we serve clients across the country with precision and care.

Our multidisciplinary team brings together civil engineers, structural consultants, architects, interior designers, and landscape specialists. Every project is an opportunity to create something that endures — technically sound and aesthetically compelling.

We are designed for small to medium-size companies and individuals willing to optimize the performance and quality of their built environments.
